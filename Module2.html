2: R/O Medical Causes

  • highlighted takeaway 1
  • highlighted takeaway 2
  • highlighted takeaway 3

Once the behavior has been described using (1) specific and (2) simple language, one needs to rule out medical explanations for challenging behaviors. Specifically, if the behavior has emerged rather suddenly, it’s possible that the behavior is more of a “symptom” or “sign” of an underlying medical condition or possible side effects of a medication change.
